# Compliance Position

Bastion Gateway is an **enablement tool**, not a guarantee of compliance.

Under the EU AI Act, record-keeping obligations under Article 12 fall on the
**provider or deployer** of a high-risk AI system — not on the infrastructure in
front of it. Bastion produces structured, signed, reconstructable decision
records that deployers can use as evidence inputs, but the deployer remains the
accountable party.

**No feature in this product makes any user compliant with any regulation.**
This is not legal advice. Consult qualified legal counsel for your specific
regulatory obligations.
